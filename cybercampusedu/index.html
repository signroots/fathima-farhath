About us

An Edu-services Group with an enviable national and international reputation, Shaheen stands tall as a leading beacon for innumerable students across India and elsewhere. Touching and transforming the many lives of students coming under its care and shaping their dreams into concrete realities, Shaheen Group is best known for its extraordinary quality coaching for NEET and other competitive/qualifying exams. Ever since its inception in 1989, owing to its innovation, learner-centric approach and irrefutable commitment Shaheen has grown exponentially: today the Group is a much acclaimed Learning Platform and a force to reckon with, in the Eduscape nationally and globally. With 50 centers across India and a little over 20000 students on roll, the Group is tirelessly marching along the path of excellence and success.
The Group has strong academic, philosophical and spiritual moorings, drawing its inspiration from the monumental works of the much celebrated poet Allama Iqbal, popularly known as The Poet of the East. Under the inspiring and dynamic leadership of Dr Abdul Qadeer, the Group aspires to orchestrate a magical transformation of the society, by empowering students with quality education, life-skills and positive orientation towards Life. Like the mythical bird Shaheen that appears in the work of Allama Iqbal, the Group constantly moves with tenacity of purpose, seeking and conquering new heights of excellence and glory.
In a nutshell, to call Shaheen as an Edu-services Group may not be fully correct.
A Movement for empowering the academic community to embrace a life of nobility, excellence and success.
